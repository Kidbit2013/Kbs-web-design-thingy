Why Build a Computer?

Building your own computer gives you better performance for your budget, easier upgrades, and a machine tailored to your needs.

Choose the Right CPU

The processor determines speed for gaming, content creation, and general multitasking. Match it with a compatible motherboard socket type.

Pick a Compatible Motherboard

Your motherboard defines the features, expansion slots, and memory support available to your build.

Do Not Skip Cooling

Good airflow and a reliable cooler keep temperatures stable, extending performance and hardware life.

Core Components

  • CPU: The brain of the computer.
  • GPU: Handles graphics and improves gaming and editing.
  • RAM: Fast memory for current tasks.
  • Storage: SSDs for speed, HDDs for capacity.
  • Case & PSU: A sturdy case plus power supply with enough wattage.
